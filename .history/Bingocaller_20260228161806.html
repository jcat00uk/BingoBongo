How to Play

  • Click Start to begin a new game.
  • Optionally, click Select Cards to manually choose player cards for AutoCheck.
  • If you manually selected cards, toggle AutoCheck ✅ on or off to automatically detect first Line and Full House.
  • Click Next to call the next number, and ↩ Undo to undo the last number.
  • Click the Select card… dropdown to check the win status of a specific card using its code.
  • Click End to finish the game.
  • If the Bingo Lingo text is visible and TTS is enabled, tap/click the text to have it read aloud.
  • Toggle Sound 🔊/🔇, TTS 🗣️/🚫, and Night Mode 🌞/🌙 using the buttons at the top of the screen.
